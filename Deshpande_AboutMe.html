About Me

Ananya Deshpande

Who do I want to be known as? The amazing and creative product mangager whose software apps changed the world. My plan for achieving my goal of becoming an amazing product manager will be maintaining a balanced relationship with my colleagues and using what I see to an advantage. A collaborative and creative mindset is the way to go! I have picked this field because I have used my creative freeedom ever since I learned to talk. Whether it meant coming up with clothing designs as a child or thinking of ways a product could have been better or easier to use. This is something I am certain I will not get bored of.

An experience I have related to the product design field is the DECA Minicon which I participated in through Dublin DECA. At the roleplay, we were assigned the role of a product manager, I decided to add that from a "previous experience", I learned that people are really into the color blue, so I designed a shoe which was blue. Just tiny things like these. Sometimes I have recieved packages from online and the first thing that popped into my mind is how it could have been more user-friendly. Maybe the packaging could have been easier or finding things on labels could have been better. This mindset is really hard to let go and is important when it comes to designing products. One thing I have realised about myself is that I am open- minded and a natural leader. I love to take charge of things and accept other people's opinions. This is what makes teamwork dreamwork. I am also constantly motivating others and myself no matter how difficlut the times get. Motivation is what keeps the work going, without motivation, people wouldn't bother even coming to work. But I want to make work a friendly and helpful environment for my colleagues.

One skill that I possess which I believe to be important is the ability to communicate. No matter what job, communication is the number one key to success. Even if it means just conversing with your colleagues. Nothing will be the way you wish for it to be one hundred percent of the time. Instead of arguing with one another, calmly conversing and figuring out the real problem is very important for the end product. Through experiences, I have realised that communication is very important for success. When we worked together and kept communicating how it was going and if we needed help, the job was that much easier. Another skill that is necessary for the role of a product manager is creativity. Coming up with new ideas is not the only job of a product manager. Taking in the views of the audience is also important. At the end of the day, whatever you are doing is not for yourself, but for the people. People like new, and new is not always easy. But I am ready for the challenge. Life is not a sweet, smooth ride, it has bumps and turns, but success will only come from overcoming these obstacles and not from giving up. To tie it all up, I am an open-minded and motivational person looking forward to becoming the product manager who changed the world. I have a mindset of how things could have been done differently or better and have the skills of communication and creativity. I thank you for taking the time to read this and invite you to explore the rest of my website. Enjoy!
