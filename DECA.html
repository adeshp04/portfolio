DECA Minicon

Located at Amador Valley High School, this was the first minicon of the year. At this event, I was in the Human Resources Management Event(HRM). The first part of this event was the roleplay. We were given a situation and five performance indicators. We had ten minutes to prepare notes and any props we would like to use during our presentation. At the time of presentation, we were restricted to only ten minutes. At the end of our presentation, we were asked questions related to the situation. To go along with my presentation, I created a graph.
