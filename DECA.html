DECA Minicon

Located at Amador Valley High School, this was the first minicon of the year. At this event, I was in the Human Resources Management Event(HRM). The first part of this event was the roleplay. We were given a situation and five performance indicators. We had ten minutes to prepare notes and any props we would like to use during our presentation. At the time of presentation, we were given ten minutes. When the presentation was over, we were asked questions related to the situation by our judge. To go along with my presentation, I created a graph of success rate. While I was presenting, I was using whatever I could find next to me, even a pen. When I was first given the situation, I analyzed the role I was given and what the company expected. These performances are beneficial because they are improvisations and involve adapting. They also make you think and release your creative side. It was easy to elaborate on the performance indicators (PI's) because of previous experiences. I was able to connect events from the past as an advantage. Because we were given ten minutes to present, you had to go on for as long as you could. Obviously no one is prepared for the entire ten minutes, but this is where the improvisation came in. You had to reconnect points and furtherly elaborate to details, and amidst this, you would recall something amazing to say. Overall, this experience was informational and a great experience. I will definitely be going to the next minicon.
