Violin

I gained interest in the violin since a young age, part of the reason being that my dad is a musician. I grew up seeing him play the harmonium and sing. When I told my dad that I wante to learn the violin, he immediately enrolled me in a class and bought me a violin. For about a year, I learned how to play the violin, but I felt like I wasn't getting where I wanted to be with it. So we switched classes and started with the technical aspect. It was a bit easier since I has previous experience. I have been playing the violin since 2016 and aim to continue.
